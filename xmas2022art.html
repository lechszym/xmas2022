How to be a pirate

Having to do quite a bit of driving, we listened (as always) to "How to train your Dragon" read by David Tennant. We managed to get through the first (How to train your Dragon) and the second (How to be a Pirate) audio-book. And so, kids drew characters from the book. On the left is Iza's rendering of Hiccup, Fishlegs and Toothless in Hopeful Puffin (that's the name of the boat) from "How to be a Pirate" (Stoick the Vast is swimming in the water). Then we have a portraits of Toothless and Hiccup by Julian.
