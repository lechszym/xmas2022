Xmas 2021

Tophouse Historic Inn

Dec 23, 2021

The Tophouse Historic Inn rents out cabins near St. Arnaud village. We arrived in the evening, a touch disappointed that the Alpine Lodge, the only restaurant in town, was closed for the holidays. This was our one night rest before the big Christmas tramp. Three-quarters of the adults got busy with the laundry, packing and re-packing, while Jacek slaved in the kitchen (half naked, for it was rather hot) cooking a delicious curry that went rather well with the glass of Roaring Meg Pinot Noir we managed to buy in a Four Square on the way. The kids took on the all important task of enjoying themselves in the Inn's garden.

If you look carefully, right at the beginning of the video below, you can see Jacek cooking (in the cabin window in the background, towards right).
