Christchurch

Jan 4-6, 2022

Christchurch is a great place to finish tramping vacation. We spent our last two days of the entire trip there, walking the parks and the city centre, which is gorgeous in places, and remarkably, all these years since the earthquake, quite hideous in numerous spots. Still, overall, it's a great place to relax and enjoy good food.

The highlight of this trip was Casa Publica a South American food restaurant we dined in. It's very close to Margaret Mahy playground. The playground is incredible and kids simply adore it (note for self - bring swimming suits next time to enjoy the water park). At the restaurant they serve guacamole fresh to the point of being made in front of your eyes, right at your table. If you sit outside (as we did) you can touch (literally) the tram passing by every 15 or 20min.

Another favourite of ours is the Riverside Market, which has everything you need, food-wise - burgers, sushi, salads, candy, shakes, beer, whisky, wines, cheese, more sweets, coffee, jams, meets (if you're into that kind of thing), and delicious, delicious pastries. It gets packed around lunch-time, so we had to take our food out and eat by the river. But then we went in again later on when it was much less busy and even more enjoyable.

This video is a capture of a running joke we had since New Year's Day on Charlotte Track. While in the tent, being very tired, dad debated out loud with himself, whether he's got the strength to get up and brush teeth before bed. Iza, in a very confrontational tone, said then "Do you want to brush them? Then brush them!". We have asked her to repeat this many times since then. This is one last time, in front of the Riverside Market, that we asked her to do it, to capture it on video for posterity.
